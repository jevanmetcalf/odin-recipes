Ingredients

  • 1 cup cheese (cubed)
  • 1/3 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup sweet pickle relish
  • 2 tablespoons - green onion (chopped)
  • 1/2 teaspoon - prepared mustard
  • 4 hot dog buns
  • 1 Sliced green olives - optional

Directions
